Welcome to the Pearl of Africa The Pearl of Africa Tour 2021

The Pearl of Africa Tour is an annual tourism event in the East African region organized by the Uganda Tourism Board (UTB). The tour gives tourists the opportunity to marvel at all the indigenous wildlife and see rare species not seen anywhere in the world at no cost.
