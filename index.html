The Pearl of Africa Tour 2021

A multicultural country in East Africa whose diverse landscape encompasses the snowcapped Rwenzori Mountains and immense Lake Victoria - the principal source of the great River Nile. With abundant wildlife such as Chimpanzees, Gorillas, famous Savannah parks, and 1000+ rare bird species.

    Explore four of East Africa's Great Lakes--Lake Victoria, Lake Kyoga, Lake Albert, and Lake Edward and more

    Explore over 20 primate species containing the highest density in all of Africa with a total of 13 including the recently discovered Dwarf Galago

    Explore the source of the Nile which stood as one of the great geographical mysteries of the Victorian Age and was alluded to hazily in the ancient writings of Ptolemy

  • Bird Paradise

    Explore the richest birding destinations on the continent with over half of all bird species in Africa
